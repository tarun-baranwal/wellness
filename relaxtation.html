Student Relaxation Guide

Simple techniques to reduce stress and improve focus

Feeling overwhelmed?

Academic pressure can be challenging. Try these research-backed techniques to regain your calm and improve productivity.

Sound Therapy

Listen to soft music, nature sounds, or binaural beats to reduce cortisol levels.

Expressive Writing

Journal your worries for 5-10 minutes daily to process emotions and clear your mind.

Movement Breaks

Take 5-minute walking breaks every hour to boost circulation and mental clarity.

Sleep Hygiene

Maintain consistent sleep schedule - 7-9 hours improves memory consolidation.

Social Connection

Talk to friends or mentors - social support reduces perceived stress.

Quick Breathing Exercise
