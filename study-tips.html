Effective Study Techniques

Science-backed methods to enhance your learning process

Active Recall

Test yourself on the material without looking at your notes to strengthen memory.

Best for: Exam preparation

Spaced Repetition

Review information at increasing intervals to combat the forgetting curve.

Best for: Long-term retention

Interleaving

Mix different topics or subjects during study sessions to improve discrimination.

Best for: Problem-solving skills

Elaboration

Explain concepts in your own words and connect them to what you already know.

Best for: Understanding complex topics

Dual Coding

Combine verbal and visual information to create multiple memory pathways.

Best for: Visual learners

Pomodoro Technique

Work in focused 25-minute intervals with 5-minute breaks to maintain concentration.

Best for: Maintaining focus

Pomodoro Timer

Try the Pomodoro technique right now. Work for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. After four cycles, take a longer 15-30 minute break.
